Efficient Fibre Optic Network Quality Assurance

The power of Automated Robotic and OTDR Measurements

Agility is a determining factor during the deployment of optical fibre, both contractors and operators need to have these networks in operation as soon as possible so that the countries of the European Union can meet the objectives defined in the Digital Agenda. It is for this reason that the use of robotics has become a key factor, thus increasing the speed, assertiveness, and safety. If the use of OTDR is combined with robotics, the efficiency and accuracy of the fibre deployment process can be substantially improved, thus reducing measurement time and costs.

Seeing the existing need in the sector, TKF has developed a platform that allows to automate the analysis of the OTDR measurement results using a cloud-based universal analysis engine. Regardless of the OTDR equipment used, CORS is a collaborative tool that provides you with accurate and automated reporting for your fibre network measurements. With the standard SOR-File format, CORS generates three reports always in the same format, consolidating all network information in one place for easy management and control.

Robotic Measuring System

A SMART time saving solution

The use of the RMS (Robotic Measuring System) and CORS solutions offers numerous advantages for fibre roll-out projects. They reduce the time required for measurement, analysis, and processing of results. They also minimize human intervention, which helps to standardize the process and the generation of reports. With a structured information flow, complete visibility, and control of progress during the whole process, the RMS and CORS solutions offer a comprehensive overview of all tests performed, with and without the RMS, and provide statistics and reports of the results.
